EC2x Overview

Quectel EC2X is based Qualcomm MDM9x07 Chip. CPU & O.S

ARMv7 Cortex A7 up to 1.2GHz with 256KB L2 cache. (Performance: 2280 DMIPS @1.2GHz, 1.9 DMIPS/MHz).

Linux distribution with kernel 3.18.
